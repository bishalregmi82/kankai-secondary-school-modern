About

A public institution rooted in Surunga and built for the future.

Kankai Secondary School is a pioneer community school in Surunga, Kankai Municipality, Jhapa. It serves learners from the town and surrounding villages, with programs from early grades through secondary level and an inclusive pathway for hearing-impaired students.
